About Me

Hello! My name is Arshdeep Singh Sahota. I was born in New Delhi, India in 2006 and moved to Canada in 2013. In 2018, I proudly became a Canadian citizen.

I've had some unique experiences, including being featured twice in the Brockville Recorder and Times newspaper — once for a summer camp and once for a culture sharing event. I also earned a worldwide first place podium finish in one of the historical events in Dirt Rally 2, one of my favorite rally games, competing against players from across the globe.
